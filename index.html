Our Story

In late 2019, just before the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic, a group of three friends came together to launch a restaurant. For nearly a year, their dream thrived until the pandemic forced them to close.

After a period of reflection, on August 16, 2024, a new food court opened. Mohammad Shariful Islam became the founder and owner, while MD Ariful Islam, as co-founder and producer, played a pivotal role in shaping the business.

Under their leadership, Mohammad Shariful Islam took charge of daily operations and overall management, while MD Ariful Islam focused on creative design, market pricing, and strategic development.

Today, the food court stands strong, thriving on their dedication and vision, delivering exceptional culinary experiences every day.
